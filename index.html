Intro

I am currently a third year attending the University of California, Berkeley. I am pursuing a major in Cognitive Science and minor in Computer Science. My inspiration into the field began with my first computer; I became somewhat of an enthusiast in the advancement of computer hardware and decided to study the science behind it. As of now, my interests have veered towards software engineering and subfields such as networking and security.

I grew up in Hayward, California which is a relatively low-income area within the Bay Area. Technology will likely play a huge role in shaping the Bay Area and I'd eventually like to be able to contribute to the community that shaped my childhood. However, that currently seems like a task which would be a bit far-off in the future.
